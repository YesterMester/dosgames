History and Evolution of SimTower

SimTower: The Vertical Empire was first released in 1995 by Maxis and developed by OpenBook Co., Ltd. The game allowed players to build and manage a skyscraper, making decisions to optimize its performance.

Over time, it gained a cult following due to its engaging gameplay and unique concept of vertical living.

Initially, SimTower was a DOS game, illustrating a shift from traditional gaming to broader digital experiences. It set the stage for future simulation games by influencing titles like The Sims and SimCity.

The online version lets players experience this classic game in new settings, fostering a community of enthusiasts who keep the spirit of the game alive.
