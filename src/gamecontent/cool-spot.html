History of Cool Spot

Cool Spot is a platform game first released in 1993 by Virgin Games. It was created for various systems, including the Mega Drive, Super Nintendo, and more.

The game is based on the character, Cool Spot, who is the mascot for the soft drink brand 7 Up.

Originating from a successful marketing campaign, the character Cool Spot became popular during the late 1980s. The game features him in a vibrant world where players help him rescue his fellow Spots. The gameplay combines platforming with colorful graphics typical of the early '90s.

Cool Spot is notable for its catchy music and engaging design. It includes various levels filled with puzzles and enemies, keeping players entertained. Its unique design features, like Cool Spot sporting sunglasses, made it stand out.

The game received positive reviews and remains a nostalgic favorite. Players appreciated its fun gameplay and bright aesthetics.

Over the years, Cool Spot has been made available online, allowing a new generation to experience this classic.
